Who We Are

WE DON'T
WHISPER.
WE MAKE ELAAN.

Elaan is an Urdu word for an announcement — the kind that turns heads, fills rooms, and gets remembered the next morning. That's the only kind of marketing we make.

We are an independent media agency out of Mohali, Punjab — wired for the Hindi belt, fluent in the regional pulse, and built around 17+ years of selling & marketing (12 of those in radio & media, the rest in BFSI) across radio, television, print and outdoor.

We don't sell inventory. We sell outcomes. A real outcome looks like a Tier-2 brand becoming a Tier-1 conversation. A regional bank getting national recall. A hospital's reels seen by every patient before they Google a doctor.

That happens when buying, planning, creative, on-ground execution and influencer reach all sit at one table. At Elaan, that table is small, sharp and accountable.

We are the agency clients call when budgets are tight and ambitions aren't. The agency that shows up with a media plan and an audit promise on the same email.

Our purpose is simple — when an Elaan brand opens its mouth, the city listens. When the city listens, the number moves.

No fluff. No filler. Just a louder version of you.

Founder's Note · 03

SINCE 2008,
ONE OBSESSION.
MEDIA THAT MOVES
THE NUMBER.

I've been selling & marketing since 2008 — twelve of those years in radio and media, the years before that with BFSI majors. That's a strange combination, and it's exactly the point.

It taught me that media is judged by one thing: did it sell? Not GRPs. Not impressions on a screenshot. Footfalls, queries, walk-ins, account openings.

Elaan is the next chapter — built on every operator relationship I've collected since. As an agency we plan and buy across BIG FM, MY FM, Radio Mirchi, Red FM, Radio City & Radio Orange, plus TV, print, OOH, digital and live. One desk. One number to call.
